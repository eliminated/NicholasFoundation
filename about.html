About TNF

Built on Principles.
Driven by Purpose.

We are engineers, researchers, and dreamers united by the belief that technology — built openly and responsibly — can transform humanity's future.

Our Vision

A World Empowered
by Open Technology

We envision a future where every human being — regardless of geography, income, or infrastructure — has access to powerful, privacy-respecting digital systems that enhance their lives.

Our Mission

Build. Open. Share.
Repeat.

TNF's mission is to design and deploy transformative technology systems, share them freely with the world, and partner with organizations committed to equitable, responsible innovation.

Our Journey

From Idea to
Global Infrastructure

2015
Foundation Established

TNF was founded with a simple premise: build technology that serves humanity, not shareholder returns. The first team of 8 engineers began work on an open connectivity toolkit.

2017
First Global Deployment

Our offline-first platform reached 500,000 users across sub-Saharan Africa, powering healthcare and education apps in areas with limited internet connectivity.

2019
AI Research Division Launched

We established our AI & ML research division, publishing federated learning research that has since been cited over 4,200 times in peer-reviewed journals.

2021
Blockchain Identity Protocol

Our decentralized identity standard was adopted by three national governments and the UN Digital ID initiative, giving identity to over 800,000 people globally.

2023
100M Lives Milestone

TNF systems directly impacted 100 million lives across 48 countries through partner integrations, open deployments, and platform APIs.

2026
Next Frontier: AGI Safety

We launched our AGI Safety & Alignment research track, partnering with 12 leading institutions to ensure advanced AI development remains beneficial and auditable.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔓

Radical Openness

Everything we build is open-source. Our code, data, research, and methodologies are free for anyone to inspect, use, and build upon.

🌍

Global Equity

We design for the world's most underserved communities first. If it works at the margins, it scales everywhere.

🧬

Scientific Rigor

We publish our work, welcome peer review, and hold ourselves to the highest standards of research and engineering quality.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

User privacy is not a feature — it is a fundamental requirement of every system we build, from architecture to deployment.

🤝

Collaborative Spirit

We believe the best outcomes emerge through partnership. We co-create with communities, not for them.

♻️

Long-term Thinking

We measure our success in decades, not quarters. Every decision is weighed against its impact on future generations.
