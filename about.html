Our Journey

From a Classroom
to the Future

2025
The Origin — Class Monitor Days

It all started in Form 5 (11th grade) in Malaysia, where our founder, Nick Nicholas Isaac, served as class monitor. What began as signing permission requests for classmates sparked a bigger vision — what if systems could be built to help people at scale?

Jan 2026
TruXSocial — First Product

The Nicholas Foundation's first product, TruXSocial, entered development. Built from scratch using PHP + Node.js coexistence architecture, the social platform reached near-polished state in just one month.

Mar 2026
DateX Sheet — Expanding the Portfolio

Work began on DateX Sheet, a high-performance spreadsheet powered by a Rust engine compiled to WebAssembly. The polyglot architecture was designed for 60fps canvas rendering and sub-millisecond recalculation.

Apr 2026
Nerd Companion — Study Meets Innovation

Nerd Companion launched development as a dedicated study app, featuring Pomodoro timers, intelligent day-load scoring, and a gamified streak system — built with Expo SDK 54 and React Native.

May 2026
Nyx AI — Entering the AI Frontier

With demand growing, development began on Nyx AI — a rule-based AI assistant built in C++20 with plans to evolve into a competitive machine learning model. Nyx features a C++ engine, a WPF desktop GUI, JSON-line protocol, and a full organizer suite.

Aug 2026
Next Chapter — University of Arizona

Our founder enrolls at the University of Arizona, bringing The Nicholas Foundation into its next phase — expanding the team, scaling the products, and continuing to engineer the future from both Malaysia and the United States.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔓

Radical Openness

Everything we build is open-source. Our code, data, research, and methodologies are free for anyone to inspect, use, and build upon.

🌍

Global Equity

We design for the world's most underserved communities first. If it works at the margins, it scales everywhere.

🧬

Scientific Rigor

We publish our work, welcome peer review, and hold ourselves to the highest standards of research and engineering quality.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

User privacy is not a feature — it is a fundamental requirement of every system we build, from architecture to deployment.

🤝

Collaborative Spirit

We believe the best outcomes emerge through partnership. We co-create with communities, not for them.

♻️

Long-term Thinking

We measure our success in decades, not quarters. Every decision is weighed against its impact on future generations.

The Builder

Meet the Founder

The Nicholas Foundation is a one-person operation today — and we're looking for talented people to join the mission.

Nick Nicholas Isaac
Founder & CEO

19-year-old developer and entrepreneur from Malaysia, currently finishing his foundation studies and enrolled to join the University of Arizona in August 2026. Nick founded The Nicholas Foundation during Form 5 (11th grade), where his role as class monitor — signing permission requests for his classmates — planted the seed for building systems that help people at scale.

One year later, the unofficial company evolved from its humble origins into a technology-first venture developing four products: TruXSocial (built in ~1 month), DateX Sheet, Nerd Companion, and Nyx AI. Nick is the sole developer, designer, and architect behind every product — writing PHP, TypeScript, Rust, C++, C#, and React Native across the entire stack.
