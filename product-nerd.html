Core Features

Study Smarter
Stay Focused

🍅

Pomodoro Timer

SVG progress ring with configurable work/break/rounds, auto-advance between phases, and automatic session tracking. Focus mode redirects to journal on completion.

📊

Day Load Score

An intelligent formula that weighs heavy subjects, detects break gaps, and scores your day's study load. Warns when you're overloading and suggests rest when needed.

🧠

Smart Scheduling

"Recommend me!" uses the Phase 2 scheduler for single sessions and full week planning — with overlap detection, preferred-window awareness, and fallback slot suggestions.

📓

Journal & Revisit

Post-session journaling with mood tracking, topic coverage notes, revisit items, and free-form reflection. A revisit queue surfaces unresolved topics for future study.

📅

Timetable & Subjects

Colour-coded subject management with icon pickers, weight priorities, and weekly hour targets. Visual timetable grid with day columns, time axis, and positioned session blocks.

🏆

Nerd Score & Streak

A gamified scoring system that rewards consistent study habits. Daily streak tracking, rotating study tips, and stat cards showing today/week/streak progress at a glance.
